    ❓ Help & Resources

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I reset my password?

    Use the Feedback form or email support@neurallearn.ai. We'll send a reset link within minutes.

    How do I access course materials?

    Navigate to Courses, select a course, and browse the syllabus. Click Enroll to get started.

    How does the AI chatbot work?

    It uses NLP to answer questions about your courses and topics. Ask anything — it knows Python, Calculus, Web Dev, ML, Docker, Git, SQL, and more!

    How are points and badges earned?

    Complete quizzes, enroll in courses, upload content, and submit feedback to earn points and unlock badges.

    Can I use NeuralLearn offline?

    Yes! Actions are queued and synced automatically when you reconnect.

    What is the Pomodoro timer?

    The study timer uses the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes of focused study followed by a 5-minute break.
